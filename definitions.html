Definitions

Branemark definition

“Osseointegration is a direct contact - on light microscopic level- between living bone and implant ”.

Current definition

“An implant is considered as osseointegrated if it doesn't have progressive relative movement with the bone at his interface ”.

Since Branemark's initial observation, osseointegration has been studied at multiple levels and recent researches are interested in the comprehension of biological mechanisms that take place and activate the healing process that brings to the osseointegration of the implant.
A better understanding of this matter leads to improve the knowledge and requirements that endosseous implants must satisfy.
