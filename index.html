Welcome to our website! Our project is dedicated to exploring osseointegration, a key concept in biomaterials. Here, you will find information about the definition of osseointegration, evaluation techniques, challenges in this field, and the latest research and technologies that are shaping the future of biomaterials.

Branemark definition

“Osseointegration is a direct contact—on light microscopic level—between living bone and implant.”

Current definition

“An implant is considered as osseointegrated if it doesn't have progressive relative movement with the bone at its interface.”
